I have 9+ years of experience in integration, state estimation, sensor fusion, SLAM, \href{https://github.com/li9i/fsm-lo}{localisation}, \href{https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10846-019-01086-y}{autonomous navigation}, \href{https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/00207179.2018.1514129}{control}, perception, \href{https://github.com/li9i/pandora\_vision\_2014}{computer vision}, and troubleshooting on real hardware. I am able to conduct research independently and materialise it into real-world development and deployment, with my aim being to bridge the two worlds in order to deliver cutting-edge systems with repeatable behaviours.
